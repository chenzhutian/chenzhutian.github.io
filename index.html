Home

I am Zhu-Tian Chen (陈竹天), currently a PostDoc at the Design Lab and Creativity Lab of UC San Diego with Prof. Haijun Xia. I received my PhD in CSE in 2020 from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where I worked with Prof. Qu Huamin at the HKUST VisLab.

My research interests are in Information Visualization, Human-Computer Interaction, and Augmented Reality. Specifically, I have been working on the visualization and interaction of information in AR environments.

I love little cats and big dogs.
